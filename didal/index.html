About us

At Adiko Kevinah Enterprises, we are dedicated to revolutionizing the agricultural landscape and addressing pressing issues faced by our local farmers and communities. Nestled in the heart of Oyam and neighboring districts, we have established ourselves as a key player in the entire agricultural value chain, providing innovative solutions that tackle challenges related to climate change, fake seeds, and malnutrition.

Our Commitment to Quality Seeds: We specialize in the production of quality declared seeds, with a particular emphasis on climate-smart varieties. Our seeds, including the renowned mak soy 3N and 6N, as well as sesame seeds, are carefully cultivated to withstand diverse climatic conditions. We take pride in offering reliable seeds that empower local farmers to thrive despite challenges like drought and flooding.

Grain Production and Value Addition: Beyond seeds, we engage in the cultivation of white and yellow maize, ensuring a stable and diverse grain supply. Taking a step further, we integrate value addition through our milling operations. This allows us to produce an array of products, including soy millet, ground nuts, rice, lentils, and tamarinds.

Nutrient-Rich Products for Holistic Well-being: Our product line extends to food supplements and whole meals designed to combat malnutrition, a prevalent issue in Oyam district. We have curated a selection of yellow maize, millet, and white maize products, carefully blended to create nutritionally rich meals suitable for the entire family, especially targeting the needs of the elderly, pregnant women, and those seeking a balanced diet.

Immunity Boosting for Healthier Communities: What sets our products apart is the incorporation of a specially formulated immunity booster. We believe in not just nourishing the body but also strengthening its defenses. Our whole meal porridge is not just a meal; it's a wellness solution that goes beyond satisfying hunger.

Empowering Communities, Ensuring Sustainability: At Adiko Kevinah Enterprises, our mission extends beyond business. We actively engage with local communities, fostering sustainable practices, and contributing to the overall well-being of our neighbors. We invite you to join us on this journey towards a healthier, more resilient future.
